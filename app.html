Voice 2.0 ✦ Setup Guide

Voice 2.0 uses w-okada — a free app that runs AI voice conversion directly on your GPU with ultra-low latency. One-time setup, then it's one click each time.

1
Download w-okada Voice Changer
Free, open-source. Click the link, find the latest Windows release (.zip), download and extract it.
github.com/w-okada — Releases
2
Download VB-Cable (free virtual mic driver)
This creates a virtual microphone on your PC so your converted voice goes to your calls — not your raw voice.
vb-audio.com/Cable
3
Configure w-okada
Open w-okada, then set:
Input → Your real microphone
Output → CABLE Input (VB-Audio Virtual Cable)
Load a voice model and press Start.
4
Select VB-Cable as your microphone
In your browser (or Discord / Zoom / OBS),
pick "CABLE Output (VB-Audio Virtual Cable)" as the microphone.
Everyone will now hear your w-okada converted voice!
GPU Required: Voice 2.0 needs a dedicated graphics card for real-time AI processing. Works best with NVIDIA GTX 1060 / AMD RX 580 or better. Integrated graphics won't work.
